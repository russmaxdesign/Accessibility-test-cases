Keyboard

  • TAB keystroke to first tab, TAB keystroke to panel, TAB keystroke to focusable items in panel, TAB keystroke out of panel
  • LEFT and/or DOWN ARROW keystroke to move to next tab. RIGHT and/or UP ARROW keystroke to move to previous tab

Screen reader

  • Announce tab - "Mercury"
  • Announce that tab is selected - "Selected"
  • Possibly Announce tab number - "1 of 5"
  • Announce panel - "Mercury".
  • Possibly announce that is is a tab panel - "Tabpanel"
